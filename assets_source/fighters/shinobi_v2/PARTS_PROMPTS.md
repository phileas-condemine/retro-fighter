# Shinobi v2 — per-part prompts (based on `references/reference_full.png`)

Design locked from the reference: black spiky messy hair, black lower-face
mask/scarf, tan bare skin on shoulders/upper arms, black segmented armor
with gold/bronze trim, brown leather chest bandolier and waist utility belt
with pouches + shuriken charm, purple waist sash with tattered trailing
ends, black tattered hakama-style pants, black armored greaves with gold
chevron trim, open ninja sandals with bare tan toes.

**Critical rule for every prompt below: neutral pose only.** The reference
is a dynamic bent-knee fighting stance — use it strictly for style, color,
material and proportion. Every individual limb part must be drawn straight
and hanging/standing neutral (not bent, raised, or striding), otherwise the
rig can't repose it. Attach `reference_full.png` to each prompt.

Common suffix for every prompt (not repeated below): *"Transparent
background, no shadow, no ground, no text, no other body parts, isolated
single piece only, same art style/proportions/lighting as the reference,
character facing right."*

---

> Generate ONLY the back half of this character's hairstyle — the mass of

> Same messy black spike texture and shading as the reference. Do NOT
> include the face, ears, forehead, or the front hair fringe (that's a
> separate piece) — hair silhouette only.
